## Strategic Pillars

### Pillar 1: Visibility & Intelligence (Months 1-3)
**Objective**: Make data visible and actionable

**Initiatives**:
1. **Analytics Dashboard** (Month 1)
   - Job category growth charts
   - Life context/interest tracking
   - Knowledge gap heatmaps
   - Top queries per category
   - Experimental vs canonical trends
   - Detection accuracy metrics

2. **Detection Accuracy Improvement** (Months 2-3)
   - Feedback mechanism for corrections
   - ML model training pipeline
   - Confidence threshold tuning
   - Multi-query context analysis

**Success Metrics**:
- Dashboard accessed daily by admin
- 5+ knowledge gaps identified per week
- 85%+ detection accuracy within 3 months
- 3+ data-driven decisions per month

### Pillar 2: Automation & Growth (Months 1-6)
**Objective**: Automate knowledge base growth

**Initiatives**:
1. **Knowledge Gap ‚Üí Research Automation** (Month 1)
   - Background job identifies gaps
   - Triggers research automatically
   - Creates experimental knowledge
   - Promotes to canonical after validation

2. **Knowledge Synthesis & Pattern Extraction** (Months 3-6)
   - Reviews top queries per category
   - Identifies common patterns
   - Extracts wisdom/insights
   - Creates W/P/G entries automatically
   - Suggests for review/promotion

**Success Metrics**:
- 50+ knowledge gaps filled automatically per month
- 20% reduction in manual research
- 10% increase in knowledge base coverage
- 30+ patterns extracted automatically per month

### Pillar 3: Marketing & Engagement (Months 2-6)
**Objective**: Improve user acquisition and engagement

**Initiatives**:
1. **Category-Specific Landing Pages** (Months 2-4)
   - Dynamic landing pages (`/for/doctors`, `/for/students`, etc.)
   - Category-specific examples
   - Success stories
   - Knowledge base preview

2. **User-Facing Analytics** (Months 4-6)
   - Personal dashboard showing query types
   - Knowledge gaps they've helped identify
   - Contribution to knowledge base
   - Personal growth metrics

**Success Metrics**:
- 20% increase in category-specific traffic
- 15% higher conversion rates
- 30% user engagement with personal analytics
- 10% increase in user retention

### Pillar 4: Intelligence & Prediction (Months 6-12)
**Objective**: Proactive assistance and cross-domain insights

**Initiatives**:
1. **Predictive Assistance** (Months 6-9)
   - ML models predict likely next queries
   - Suggest proactive help
   - Identify patterns in user behavior
   - Recommend knowledge to review

2. **Cross-Category Insights** (Months 9-12)
   - Identify similar queries across categories
   - Find transferable knowledge
   - Discover cross-domain patterns
   - Extract universal insights

**Success Metrics**:
- 25% reduction in user friction
- 15% increase in proactive assistance accuracy
- 20+ cross-category connections identified
- 10% improvement in assistant intelligence

### Pillar 5: Community & Scale (Months 9-18)
**Objective**: Leverage community for knowledge growth

**Initiatives**:
1. **Community Knowledge Sharing** (Months 9-12)
   - Users share knowledge they've created
   - Vote on knowledge quality
   - Suggest improvements
   - Contribute patterns/wisdom

2. **Advanced Analytics & Insights** (Months 12-18)
   - Predictive analytics for knowledge gaps
   - Trend analysis across categories
   - User behavior prediction
   - Knowledge quality scoring

**Success Metrics**:
- 100+ community contributions per month
- 80%+ quality score on community knowledge
- 50% reduction in manual knowledge creation
- 25% increase in knowledge base growth rate
