## ğŸš€ Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Sign Up (1 minute)

1. Visit [infinityassistant.io](https://infinityassistant.io)
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Choose your signup method:
   - **Email**: Enter your email and create a password
   - **Google**: Sign in with Google (one-click)
   - **Anonymous**: Start immediately (no account needed)

### Step 2: Choose Your Product (1 minute)

After signing up, you'll see the **Product Selector**:

- **Infinity Assistant**: AI chat assistant for questions and help
- **Infinity Builder**: AI-powered code builder and generator

**Choose one** (you can switch later in settings)

### Step 3: Complete Onboarding (2 minutes)

The onboarding wizard will guide you through:

1. **Welcome** - Introduction to Infinity Assistant
2. **Features** - What you can do
3. **Workspace** (Optional) - Connect your workspace
4. **Builder** (If selected) - Builder mode introduction
5. **Quick Start** - First steps
6. **Complete** - You're ready!

**Tip**: You can skip onboarding and come back later

### Step 4: Start Chatting (1 minute)

1. Type your first message in the chat
2. Press Enter or click Send
3. Get instant AI-powered responses!

**Example messages**:
- "What is artificial intelligence?"
- "Help me write a Python function"
- "Explain quantum computing"
